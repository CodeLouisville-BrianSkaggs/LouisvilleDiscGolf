Welcome!

Louisville, KY has a rich disc golf tradition dating back to the 1970s. The purpose of this website is to serve as an information guide for the various disc golf courses in and around Louisville.

Course Designs and Reviews

Interested in playing some disc golf in the Louisville area? Check out the COURSES section to see which course is right for you.

Already played a course? We'd love to hear your opinion. Visit the COURSES section to leave a review for future disc golfers.

New to Disc Golf?

Disc golf is a wonderful outdoor activity that is extremely affordable, easy to learn, and accommodates all ages and fitness levels.

Visit the WHAT IS DISC GOLF? section to learn about the history and rules of the game.
